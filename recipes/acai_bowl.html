Acai Bowl

Description

Açaí bowls are vibrant and nutritious breakfast dishes made from blended açaí berries, frozen fruits, and a splash of liquid. Topped with an array of fresh fruits, crunchy granola, and other delights, these bowls offer a delicious way to fuel your day with antioxidants and flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup frozen acai puree, slightly thawed
  • ¼ cup liquid (like your milk of choice or fruit juice of choice)
  • 1 banana
  • ½ cup fruit of choice (like strawberries, blueberries, and pineapple)

Toppings

  • Granola
  • Honey
  • Peanut butter or nut butter
  • Fresh fruit (like strawberries, blueberries, pineapple, banana, blackberies, and raspberries)
  • Shredded coconut
  • Chia seeds
  • Nuts
  • Chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Slightly thaw the frozen acai puree, either by soaking it in warm water for a few minutes or placing it in the refrigerator overnight.
  2. Add acai, your liquid of choice, banana, and any other fruits to a high-speed blender.
  3. Blend until all the frozen chunks are gone and the mixture is thick and smooth.
  4. Spoon the mixture into a bowl, top with your desired toppings, and serve immediately.
